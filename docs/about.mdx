**FoBE Studio**, established in 2020, is a technology firm specializing in environmental technology, IoT communications, electrochemical sensing, air quality monitoring, and big data analytics.
We are dedicated to offering comprehensive, one-stop IoT solutions that encompass hardware design, software development, and cloud platform architecture.

Our mission is to empower clients in their digital transformation and intelligent upgrading endeavors through cutting-edge technologies and expert services.
Having amassed extensive experience across various sectors, we have forged enduring partnerships with numerous distinguished enterprises.

We remain committed to our core principles of innovation, professionalism, and efficiency, ensuring the delivery of superior services and solutions to our clientele.
