## Introduction

The **FoBE Breakout BM8563** integrates the [GATEMODE BM8563](https://www.makerguides.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/BM8563-datasheet.pdf) real-time clock/calendar IC, providing complete date and time information with automatic calendar calculations including leap years. The clock operates in both 12-hour and 24-hour modes with full binary-coded decimal (BCD) format. Multiple connectivity options are provided for easy integration into various applications.

## Applications

- Security access controllers and door controllers
- Time recorders and attendance systems
- IC water-flow meters and gas meters

## Key Features

**BM8563**
