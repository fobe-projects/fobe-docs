## Introduction

The **FoBE Breakout GP8303** integrates the [Linearin GP8303](https://atta.szlcsc.com/upload/public/pdf/source/20240407/08D5255A2F15F1A198C1992E12D1CC6A.pdf) I2C to 4-20mA DAC converter and the EEPROM for calibration data storage. The module converts 12-bit digital values (0x000-0xFFF) to precise 4-20mA analog current output with excellent linearity of 0.1%. Multiple connectivity options are provided for easy integration into various applications.

## Applications

- Function signal generators and test equipment
- Automatic controllers for lighting and fountain systems
- Industrial automation and process control
- Electromechanical equipment debugging and calibration
- Small-scale testing and measurement devices
