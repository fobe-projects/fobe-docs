## Features

### IIC (JST SH 1.0mm Connector)

- Standardized 4-Pin connector, foolproof design, quick assembly.
- Multiple modules of sensors, networks, accessories, continuously expanding.
- Cross-platform compatible: Adafruit STEMMA & STEMMA QT, SparkFun Qwiic / e-Radionica EasyC / Modulino Comparison.
- A single standardized size makes prototyping simpler and faster.

### MFP (JST SH 1.0mm Connector)

- Standardized 6-Pin connector, foolproof design, quick assembly.
- Multiple modules of sensors, networks, accessories, continuously expanding.
- Can be expanded into two IIC (4-Pin) connections.
- A single standardized size makes prototyping simpler and faster.
